Quesadilla Recipe

Quesadillas are a classic in Cole's kitchen. Simple, quick, easy, they're hard to beat. Not to mention there are a lot of ways to customize them and make them better. Today, I'll give you my recipe for a basic cheese quesadilla because I am in college and have no money for fancy stuff like steak

What you'll need:

  • 2 tortillas
  • 3 cups of a cheese of your choice
  • salsa (optional)
  • guacamole (optional)

Steps:

  1. Pick out a large pan, one that could fit one of the tortillas in it.
  2. Put the pan over the stove and turn it to medium heat.
  3. Place one of the tortillas on the pan, making sure it is flat to the bottom.
  4. Put as much cheese of your choosing as your heart desires onto the tortilla. Just make sure it doesn't exceed the height of the pan. That will not end well.
  5. Place the second tortilla on top of the cheese.
  6. Watch for the cheese to melt. Once about half the cheese melts, place a plate face down over the pan. Hold the plate with one hand and flip the pan so that the quesadilla is now on the plate.
  7. Slowly slide the quesadilla back into the pan so the other side can cook.
  8. Once the cheese is fully melted, place the quesadilla back on the plate.
  9. Cut up the quesadilla with scissors. This is the spot where you realize that putting too much cheese may have been a mistake.
  10. Pour the salsa and the guacamole into their own dipping bowls.
  11. Enjoy!
