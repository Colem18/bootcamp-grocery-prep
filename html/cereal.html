Cereal

Everyone knows and loves cereal. Whether you just woke up and need an easy breakfast or you're craving something sweet throughout the day, cereal is a wonderful choice. There are a ton of different kinds of cereals and almost everyone has a favorite, so cereal discourse is one of the best ways to get to know someone on a personal level.

What you'll need:

Servings

1
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 3 cups of a cereal of your choice

Steps:

  1. Get your bowl out.
  2. These next few steps are very important and accidentally mixing up the order could be catasrophic, so pay very close attention.
  3. Make sure the bowl is empty. No liquids in the bowl. Nada. Zip. Zilch.
  4. Pour your cereal into the bowl.
  5. Now that your cereal is in the bowl, you can pour the milk in. Again, make sure the cereal is in the bowl before you pour in the milk
  6. Enjoy your cereal now that it has been prepared correctly!
