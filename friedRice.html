Fried Rice Recipe

Fried rice is a great because it isn't too hard to make, but it still produces a delicious, well-rounded meal. There are many different kinds of fried rice and its almost up to the chef ingredients to use, but we'll stick to a simple egg fried rice to start you off.

What you'll need:

  • 3 cups of jasmine rice (best if made the day before and stored in the fridge)
  • 3 or 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of peas
  • 1/2 cup of diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup of chopped green beans
  • 1/2 of diced broccoli florets
  • 1 Lime
  • 3 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of cooking oil

Steps:

  1. Place a pan on the stove and turn the heat on medium.
  2. Drop a splash of cooking oil into the pan.
  3. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until they are fully mixed.
  4. Pour your eggs into the pan and watch them cook, making sure to scramble them from time to time.
  5. When the eggs are fully cooked, place them in a bowl or on a plate to use later.
  6. Add another splash of cooking oil.
  7. Put your rice into the pan and break it apart with a spatula.
  8. Once the oil is relatively evenly distributed among the rice, add in a couple splashes of fish sauce and a splash of soy sauce. Flavor to taste.
  9. Pour your vegetables into the pan and mix the rice until the mixture seems uniform -ish.
  10. Put the eggs back into the pan and mix the rice again until the mixture is uniform-ish once.
  11. Once the rice is done being mixed, slice the lime and squeeze its juice onto the rice, making sure to cover as much of it as possible.
  12. Go over the rice with a pepper shaker, adding as much pepper as you like.
  13. Serve and enjoy!
